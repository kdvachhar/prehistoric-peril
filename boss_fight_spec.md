# Boss Fight Specification

## Setting

At the end of Level 2, the player enters a cave. Walking into the cave transitions them into a boss room — the fight takes place inside the cave, not in the open world.

## Boss: Grand Chompy (Giant Alligator)

The alligator sits in a pond inside the cave. It has **10 hit points** and must be defeated before the player can exit.

## Attacks

### Water Balls
The alligator launches water balls that rain down from above. The player must dodge them as they fall.

### Claw Swipe
The alligator swipes a claw at the player, dealing damage if it connects.

### Tongue
The alligator shoots its sharp tongue out toward the player. This is the **only attack window** — the player must:
1. Land on the tongue while it is extended
2. Hit the alligator's mouth with the bat while standing on the tongue

Any hit outside of this mechanic does not damage the boss.

## Victory

Once the boss reaches 0 HP it dies. The player can then exit the cave to complete the level.
